About ONGC


Oil and Natural Gas Corporation (ONGC) is India's largest oil and gas exploration and production company. Founded in 1956, ONGC plays a pivotal role in the energy sector, providing over 70% of Indiaâ€™s crude oil and around 60% of its natural gas. Committed to energy security, ONGC leads in sustainable and responsible resource management..


Beyond domestic operations, ONGC has a global footprint with exploration and production activities in over 17 countries. The company's operations span both onshore and offshore domains, encompassing exploration, drilling, production, and refining of oil and natural gas. ONGC is renowned for its technological prowess and innovation in the oil and gas industry, continually pushing boundaries to enhance efficiency and sustainability. Committed to environmental stewardship and corporate social responsibility, ONGC strives to integrate best practices in resource management and community development initiatives, ensuring sustainable growth and societal impact.


Our Values


MISSION: At ONGC, we are at the forefront of innovation in the energy sector, leveraging cutting-edge machine prediction technologies to revolutionize our operations. Our mission is to harness the power of predictive analytics and machine learning algorithms to optimize exploration, production, and refining processes. By predicting equipment failures before they occur, we ensure uninterrupted operations and maximize asset performance.


VISION: Through continuous research and development, we aim to pioneer new methodologies that enhance operational efficiency and reduce environmental impact. Our commitment to machine prediction aligns with our broader vision of sustainable growth and leadership in the global energy landscape. At ONGC, we are dedicated to integrating advanced technologies that not only meet current energy demands but also pave the way for a cleaner and more sustainable future.
